Our Mission

The Data Science Association is devoted to applying data science across the STEM field, and widening the variety of people in the data science sector.

What We've Accomplished

  • Launched independent student summer research
  • Attracted over 30 members to our campus community
  • Hosted lectures on SQL and Python by Masters students and professors
  • Built a strong foundation for future growth
